# tableau-global-salary-dashboard

**Global Job & Salary Dashboard (Tableau)**
Project Summary

This project is a Global Job & Salary Dashboard created using Tableau.
It visualizes salary trends, experience levels, job titles, and company details to understand the global job market.

**Objective**

To analyze global job salary data

To compare salaries by experience level

To visualize employment type and company size

To identify top countries and job roles

**Dashboard Features**

Average Salary by Experience Level (Entry, Intermediate, Senior, Expert)

World Map â€“ Average Salary by Country

Top Employee Residence Countries

Employment Type Distribution (Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Freelance)

Company Size Distribution (Small, Medium, Large)

Average Salary by Job Title and Experience Level Table

Filters: Job Title, Company Location
**
Dataset**

The dataset includes:

Job Title

Experience Level

Salary in USD

Employment Type

Company Location

Employee Residence

Company Size

**How to Use**

Open Global_Job_Salary_Dashboard.twbx in Tableau Desktop

Use filters to select Job Title or Company Location

Explore charts, tables, and map for insights

**Key Insights**

Senior and Expert roles have the highest salaries

Most jobs are Full-time

Medium-sized companies dominate

USA and UK have the highest employee residence count
